Internships

All math, computer science, and computer information systems majors have internships supervised by faculty as part of their degree requirements. Our students hold internships at a variety of startups, nonprofits, and established organizations. Below is a list of some of the places where our students have held internships:

  • John Wiley & Sons, Inc. (Wiley)
  • Parent Media Group, Inc.
  • RVC Youth Council, Inc.
  • United Nations (Cybersecurity digital blue helmets) - after graduating
