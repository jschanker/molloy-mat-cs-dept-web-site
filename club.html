Department of Math and Computer Studies

MACS Math and Computer Studies Club

The purpose of the Math and Computer Studies Club (MaCS) is to promote the interest of mathematics and computer studies at Molloy, and to engage students, through interaction with faculty, alumni and those that are established in the industry, by applying the knowledge to research and industry. For more information, email MaCS@lions.molloy.edu.
